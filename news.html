Postdoctor Recruitment Information
Dec 26, 2022
The Peking University Chen Yuezhou Laboratory was established in November 2022 at the Peking University School of Life Sciences and the Peking University-Tsinghua Joint Center for Life Sciences. The laboratory focuses on B cell immunology and conducts research on the body's protective immunological mechanism after infection or vaccination, the formation mechanism of pathogenic autoantibodies in autoimmune diseases, and the interaction between commensal microorganisms and the host. Dr. Chen Yuezhou's recent work (first author) has been published in high-level journals such as Cell, Science Immunology, JEM, etc. Now chenlab is recruiting 1-2 postdoctoral fellows.
